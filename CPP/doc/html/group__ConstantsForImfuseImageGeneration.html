enum  AdjustModes { NoAdj , Up , Down }
 AdjustModes documentation Enumeration with the possibilities to apply a gamma transformation to obtain the output images. NoAdj mean not to apply any gamma transformation, Up means to apply a convex one (decrease low gray values, increase high gray values) and Down means to apply a concave one (increase low gray values, decrease high gray values)
